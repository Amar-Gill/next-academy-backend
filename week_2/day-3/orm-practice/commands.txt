Update the product name

>>> [(p.id, p.name) for p in Product]
[(1, 'Cool Mug'), (2, 'Mug of Ages')]
>>> new_name = 'Ultra Mug'
>>> Query = Product.update(name = new_name).where(Product.id == 1)
>>> Query.execute()
1
>>> [(p.id, p.name) for p in Product]
[(1, 'Ultra Mug'), (2, 'Mug of Ages')]
